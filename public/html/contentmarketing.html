Content Marketing

Content marketing is a marketing technique of creating and distributing valuable, relevant and consistent content to attract and acquire a clearly defined audience – with the objective of driving profitable customer action.

Content Marketing includes writing blog posts, creating a landing page for traffic to visit, writing valuable, relevant YouTube video descriptions to attract potential customers.

Content marketing consists of two stages of the buying process:

  • Raising awareness of solutions.
  • educating consumers about a product they may have never considered before.

Potential clients find your content, find value in it, and by the time they contact you, they’re already convinced they want to work with you.
